# CODEGA AI Rules

- No hallucinated project state: proje yoksa yok de.
- No premature coding: domain ve mimari netlesmeden kod yazma.
- No Sanctum/JWT confusion: Laravel icin Sanctum kullanilir; JWT ayri teknolojidir.
- No vague database design: tablolar fields, data types, relations, indexes, unique rules ve soft delete karari ile verilir.
- No generic Flutter answer: Clean Architecture core, features, data, domain, presentation, providers ve widgets ayrimiyla detaylandirilir.
- No incomplete vehicle domain: users, vehicles, traffic_insurances, casco_policies, inspections, exhaust_emissions, maintenance_records, vehicle_documents, reminders ve notifications planlanir.
- No missing reminders: 30 gun, 15 gun, 7 gun ve 1 gun kala bildirim akisi belirtilir.
- No missing deployment basics: Docker, Nginx, MySQL, Queue Worker, Scheduler/Cron ve SSL anlatilir.
- No hidden risk: security, testing, deployment ve risks ayri yazilir.
- No internal reasoning dump: kullaniciya sadece temiz final cevap gosterilir.
